Our Mission

Strategic planning to maximize profit, reduce cost, and deliver quality service.

Strategic Planning

The Ludo Consultants approach to strategic planning involves a thorough look at an organization or a service line(s) within the organization. Our consultants will complete an onsite visit in which they will:

  • Assess structure and function of the organization or service line(s)
  • Identify areas of success
  • Identify opportunities for improvement
  • Assist with the completion of a strategic plan

S.W.O.T. Analysis

A SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats) analysis is completed as part of the strategic planning process, but it can also be done independently when an organization already has a strategic plan in place and seeks a focused analysis on a specific service line.

Team Building

Team building strengthens the organization and ultimately benefits the community in which it resides. Ludo leads groups within the organization to work together effectively as a team.

We provide tools to help team members with different personalities understand one another and use their differences to create stronger, more collaborative teams.
